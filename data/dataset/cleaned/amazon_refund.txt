[DOCUMENT: Amazon.in Refund Policy]

[SECTION: Refund Trigger Conditions]
* Refund is issued only after Amazon receives the returned item, for Amazon-delivered orders.
* Refund is issued only after the seller notifies Amazon of receipt of the return, for seller-fulfilled orders.
* For pre-paid transactions, refund is issued to the original payment method.
* For Pay on Delivery orders, refund is issued to the customer's bank account via NEFT or as Amazon Pay balance.

[SECTION: Refund Timelines — Amazon Delivered Orders (Prepaid)]
* Amazon Pay Balance refund for Amazon-delivered prepaid orders is processed in up to 4 hours after return receipt.
* Credit Card or Debit Card refund for Amazon-delivered prepaid orders is processed in up to 5 working days after return receipt.
* UPI Linked Bank Account refund for Amazon-delivered prepaid orders is processed in up to 5 working days after return receipt.

[SECTION: Refund Timelines — Seller-Fulfilled Orders (Prepaid)]
* Credit Card or Debit Card refund for seller-fulfilled prepaid orders is processed in up to 5 working days after seller notifies Amazon of return receipt.
* UPI Linked Bank Account refund for seller-fulfilled prepaid orders is processed in up to 5 working days after seller notifies Amazon of return receipt.

[SECTION: Refund Timelines — Pay on Delivery Orders]
* NEFT to Bank Account refund for Amazon-delivered Pay on Delivery orders is processed in up to 5 working days after return receipt.
* NEFT to Bank Account refund for seller-fulfilled Pay on Delivery orders is processed in up to 5 working days after seller notifies Amazon of return receipt.
* Amazon Pay Balance refund for Pay on Delivery orders is processed in up to 4 hours.
* Paper Cheque refund is processed in up to 10 working days.

[SECTION: Refund Method Constraints]
* Refunds are not processed in cash under any circumstance.
* Refunds via paper cheque are issued only in exceptional cases.
* Refunds cannot be processed to third-party accounts.
* The name on the customer's Amazon account must match the name of the bank account holder for NEFT refunds.
* International customer refunds are issued only to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.

[SECTION: Seller-Fulfilled Order Refunds]
* For seller-fulfilled orders with damaged or defective items, the seller shall issue a refund only if the item cannot be repaired or replaced.
* Refund for seller-fulfilled orders is processed only after the seller authorizes the refund.
* Refund for seller-fulfilled orders is processed only after the seller notifies Amazon of receipt of the returned item.

[SECTION: Amazon Pay Later Refunds]
* If no installment has been paid for the returned product, the Amazon Pay Later limit shall be credited with the total order value.
* If installments have already been paid, the paid amount shall be credited back to the customer's bank account and the credit limit shall be reinstated.

[SECTION: EMI Refunds]
* If an EMI has already been paid, the amount paid as EMI shall be refunded to the credit card and the loan shall be cancelled.
* The card-issuing bank may charge nominal pre-closure charges if an EMI has already been paid.

[SECTION: Shipping Cost Refunds]
* Return shipping costs of up to Rs.100 are eligible for refund only for Fulfilled by Amazon and Prime Eligible item returns.
* Gift-wrapping costs are eligible for refund only for Fulfilled by Amazon and Prime Eligible item returns.
* Shipping cost refunds for Fulfilled by Amazon and Prime Eligible items are issued to the customer's Amazon Pay Balance only.
* Return shipping costs exceeding Rs.100 for large and heavy items are eligible for additional refund only if proof of payment such as a courier receipt is submitted.
* Shipping costs incurred for remorse returns are not eligible for refund from the seller.
