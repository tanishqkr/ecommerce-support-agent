DOCUMENT: PERISHABLE GOODS RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

SECTION: DEFINITIONS

Perishable goods include fresh fruits, vegetables, meats, seafood, poultry, dairy products, baked goods with shelf life under 30 days, cut or live flowers, organic produce, ready-to-eat meals, meal kits, chilled prepared foods, and frozen foods.
Damaged goods are items received in a physically broken, crushed, leaked, or structurally compromised state attributable to transit or handling.
Spoiled goods are items received in a state of biological degradation including visible mold, foul odor, discoloration, or exceeding the printed expiry date at the time of delivery.
Reporting window is the mandatory period within which a return or refund claim must be initiated by the customer, beginning at the timestamp of delivery confirmation.

SECTION: ELIGIBILITY RULES

Item is not eligible for return under standard circumstances on the basis of customer preference, change of mind, or delayed consumption.
Item is eligible for return or refund only if the item was delivered in a physically damaged condition.
Item is eligible for return or refund only if the item was delivered in a spoiled or inedible condition.
Item is eligible for return or refund only if the item delivered does not match the order in product, variant, or quantity.
Item is eligible for return or refund only if the item's printed expiry date was surpassed prior to the date of delivery.
Item is eligible for return or refund only if temperature-sensitive items were delivered outside the required cold chain specification, evidenced by packaging condition or temperature indicator failure.
Return for change of mind is not eligible for perishable goods.

SECTION: CLAIM TIMELINE

Claim must be submitted within 24 hours of delivery confirmation timestamp.
Claim submitted after 24 hours shall be automatically rejected and is not eligible for manual review.
Platform-recorded delivery timestamps shall be treated as the definitive reference for all time calculations.
Claim must be timestamped within the 24-hour window through any channel including app, website, or customer support to be considered valid.

SECTION: EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS

Claim must include a minimum of two clear photographs of the item showing the defect, damage, or spoilage with the item and its original packaging in the same frame.
Claim must include one photograph of the outer delivery packaging as received showing any external damage.
Claim must include one photograph clearly showing the product label including the expiry or best-before date and batch number.
Claim for frozen goods must include a video demonstrating spoilage characteristics such as texture, odor description, or packaging failure.
Claim submission is not eligible if evidence is incomplete; resubmission must occur within the 24-hour window.

SECTION: NON-ELIGIBILITY EXCEPTIONS

Item is not eligible for refund on grounds of aesthetic dissatisfaction, minor cosmetic variation, or natural product variation such as size, color, or shape differences within acceptable agricultural norms.
Partial spoilage of a multi-unit pack is not eligible for a full order refund; refund shall be prorated to the affected unit only.
Item is not eligible for refund if stored incorrectly by the customer after delivery, including failure to refrigerate dairy products.
Item is not eligible for refund if marked as near expiry or short shelf life at the time of listing and discounted accordingly, solely on the basis of expiry proximity.
Item is not eligible for refund if spoilage resulted from delivery delays caused by force majeure events, customer-caused failed delivery attempts, or incorrect delivery addresses.

SECTION: PROCESSING TIMELINES

Claim must be submitted within 24 hours of delivery timestamp.
Evidence review and acknowledgement shall occur within 4 business hours of claim submission.
Claim decision shall be issued within 24 business hours of evidence receipt.
Refund initiation shall occur within 2 business days of claim approval.
Refund credit to payment source shall occur within 3 to 7 business days depending on the bank.
Platform credit or coupon as an alternate refund shall be issued within 24 hours of claim approval.

SECTION: LEGAL OVERRIDE

Statutory consumer rights override platform policies.
This policy is subject to applicable consumer protection laws of the jurisdiction in which the order was delivered.
Statutory consumer rights shall prevail in cases of conflict with this policy.
