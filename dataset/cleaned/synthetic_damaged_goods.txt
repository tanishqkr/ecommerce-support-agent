DOCUMENT: DAMAGED AND DEFECTIVE GOODS POLICY

SECTION: DEFINITIONS

Damaged goods means products that sustain physical harm during transit, storage, or last-mile delivery, including broken or cracked components, crushed or torn packaging resulting in product compromise, water or moisture ingress, bent or warped structural elements, and leaking or spilled contents.
Defective goods means products that fail to function as described or represented due to a fault originating from the manufacturing, assembly, or quality control process, including failure to power on or initialize, missing factory-installed components, incorrect assembly, sub-standard materials causing premature failure under normal use, and software or firmware faults on electronic products.
Dead on arrival means an electronic or mechanical product that fails to function at all upon first attempted use without any user-induced damage.
Cosmetic damage means superficial non-functional imperfections including minor scratches, scuffs, or dents that do not impair the product's performance.
Replacement means the dispatch of a new equivalent product in lieu of a refund at the customer's election or as determined by platform policy.

SECTION: ELIGIBILITY RULES

Refund or replacement is mandatory for all verified cases of damage or defect.
Refund is not eligible to be denied where damage or defect has been confirmed through the inspection process.
Customer shall have the right to elect either a full refund or a replacement at the time of claim approval, subject to product availability.
Refund is eligible if the elected replacement product is out of stock.
Partial refund is eligible only where the customer elects to retain the damaged item and requests compensation for diminished value.

SECTION: CLAIM INITIATION RULES

Claim for damaged goods must be initiated within 48 hours of delivery for all product categories except electronics.
Claim for defective goods must be initiated within 7 days of delivery for standard products.
Claim for defective goods must be initiated within the applicable warranty period for electronics and appliances.
Return is not eligible without first initiating a claim and receiving a Return Merchandise Authorization number from the platform.
Unauthorized return submitted without a Return Merchandise Authorization number shall not be processed and may be rejected or returned to the customer.

SECTION: ELECTRONICS AND APPLIANCES SPECIAL RULES

Dead on arrival claim for electronics must be initiated within 48 hours of delivery.
Platform shall arrange a technical inspection within 3 business days of dead on arrival claim initiation.
Manufacturing defect claims for electronics outside the dead on arrival window but within the warranty period must be directed to the brand's authorized service center in the first instance.
Platform-level claim for electronics manufacturing defect is eligible only if the authorized service center confirms the defect in writing.
Item is not eligible for claim under this policy if physical damage to electronics was caused by misuse, drops, liquid ingress after delivery, or unauthorized repair.

SECTION: EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS

Claim must include a minimum of three clear photographs showing the defect or damage from different angles.
Claim must include at least one photograph showing the full product and at least one showing the specific area of damage or defect in close-up.
Claim must include photographs of the outer delivery box or packaging as received including all visible damage to packaging.
Claim must include a photograph of the product label, model number, and serial number for electronics and appliances.
Claim for electronics must include a video demonstrating the defective behavior, with a minimum duration of 30 seconds and a maximum of 3 minutes.
Platform must not reject a claim based solely on the absence of an unboxing video.
Rejection must be supported by physical inspection findings.

SECTION: NON-ELIGIBILITY EXCEPTIONS

Item is not eligible for return if cosmetic damage was pre-disclosed in the product listing including open-box or refurbished items.
Item is not eligible for return if damaged due to customer misuse, unauthorized modifications, or improper storage after delivery.
Item is not eligible for return for normal wear and tear, consumable depletion, or end-of-life performance degradation.
Digital products, software licenses, and downloadable content are not eligible for physical damage claims under this policy.
Item is not eligible for damage or defect claims if sold explicitly as For Parts Only, Non-Functional, or As-Is, provided the non-functional status was clearly disclosed prior to purchase.

SECTION: INSPECTION AND DECISION RULES

Platform must issue a Return Merchandise Authorization number within 24 business hours of claim submission, or reject the claim with a stated reason.
Platform must schedule a pickup from the delivery address within 2 business days of Return Merchandise Authorization issuance.
Inspection must be completed within 2 business days of item receipt at the facility.
Inspection shall classify the returned item as verified damaged or defective with claim approved, inconsistent with claim with partial resolution offered, or no defect found or customer-induced damage with claim rejected.
Platform must provide a written inspection report citing specific findings if the inspection finds no defect or that damage was customer-induced.
Inspection report must include the inspector's name, inspection date, and detailed observations.
In cases of dispute between customer-submitted evidence and inspection findings, the platform shall escalate to a senior quality control reviewer whose determination shall be final subject to the customer's right to escalate under the Dispute Resolution Policy.

SECTION: PROCESSING TIMELINES

Damage claim must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery.
Defect claim must be submitted within 7 days of delivery for standard products.
Return Merchandise Authorization shall be issued within 24 business hours of claim submission.
Pickup shall be scheduled within 2 business days of Return Merchandise Authorization issuance.
Physical inspection shall be completed within 2 business days of item receipt at the facility.
Claim decision shall be communicated within 1 business day of inspection completion.
Refund initiation shall occur within 2 business days of claim approval.
Replacement dispatch shall occur within 3 business days of claim approval.
Refund credit to payment source shall occur within 3 to 7 business days depending on the bank.
Claim rejection notification with inspection report shall be issued within 1 business day of inspection completion.
Customer escalation must be submitted within 15 days of rejection notification.

SECTION: LEGAL OVERRIDE

Statutory consumer rights override platform policies.
This policy operates alongside and does not restrict any statutory warranty or guarantee rights conferred by applicable consumer protection law.
Platform's liability under this policy is limited to the order value of the damaged or defective item.
