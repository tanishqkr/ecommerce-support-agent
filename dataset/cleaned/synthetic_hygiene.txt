DOCUMENT: HYGIENE-SENSITIVE PRODUCTS RETURN POLICY

SECTION: DEFINITIONS

Hygiene-sensitive products include innerwear, undergarments, lingerie, shapewear, and swimwear for all genders and age groups.
Hygiene-sensitive products include cosmetics such as foundation, lipstick, mascara, eyeliner, blush, and loose powders.
Hygiene-sensitive products include skincare products such as serums, creams, moisturizers, toners, and face masks.
Hygiene-sensitive products include haircare products such as shampoos, conditioners, hair oils, and hair color kits if opened.
Hygiene-sensitive products include personal care items such as razors, trimmers, electric shavers, toothbrushes, and oral care accessories.
Hygiene-sensitive products include feminine hygiene products and adult incontinence products.
Hygiene-sensitive products include medical-grade skincare, prescription topicals, and OTC dermatological products.
Hygiene-sensitive products include ear and eye care products including eardrops, contact lens solutions, and eye drops.
Sealed product means a product whose original factory or manufacturer seal including shrink wrap, foil seal, cap seal, or tamper-evident tape remains intact and unbroken at the time of the return request.
Manufacturing defect means a flaw or failure attributable to the manufacturing process including incorrect formulation, contaminated contents, broken applicators from production, and missing product components listed on the label.
Tampered product means a product whose seal has been broken, removed, partially detached, or shows evidence of resealing.

SECTION: ELIGIBILITY RULES

Item is not eligible for return once the original seal has been opened, broken, or tampered with, regardless of the reason for the return request.
Return is eligible only if the product seal is fully intact and unbroken at the time of return initiation.
Return is eligible only if the return is initiated within 7 days of the delivery date.
Return is eligible only if the product is in its original packaging with all components, inserts, and accessories included.
Return is eligible only if the return reason is a manufacturing defect, wrong product delivered, damaged in transit, or expired product delivered.
Return is eligible if the product contains a verifiable manufacturing fault that renders it unfit for use, present prior to opening and visible through sealed packaging or discovered upon first intended use without prior misuse.
Return is eligible if the product delivered does not match the product ordered in terms of item name, variant, shade, size, or SKU.
Return is eligible if the product packaging or container was physically damaged during delivery, compromising product integrity.
Return is eligible if the product's expiry or use-by date had already elapsed at the time of delivery.

SECTION: SEAL AND TAMPER CONDITIONS

Item is not eligible for return if there is any evidence of seal tampering including partial peeling, re-application of tape, puncture marks, or label removal.
Item returned without intact seal shall be automatically rejected and returned to the customer at the customer's shipping cost.
Platform reserves the right to inspect returned products and reject returns where tampering is identified after collection.

SECTION: NON-ELIGIBILITY EXCEPTIONS

Manufacturing defect claim is eligible for an opened product only if the defect could not have been identified without opening, such as contaminated product, broken internal applicator, or incorrect fill quantity.
Such claims for opened products must be supported by clear photographic or video evidence.
Allergic reactions or skin sensitivities alone are not eligible as return grounds under this policy.
Item is not eligible for return if innerwear or swimwear was tried on without the protective hygiene liner intact.

SECTION: EVIDENCE REQUIREMENTS

Claim must include one photograph clearly showing the intact seal of the product.
Claim must include a minimum of two photographs clearly showing the defect, damage, or discrepancy forming the basis of the claim.
Claim must include a photograph of the product label showing batch number, expiry date, and manufacturing details.
Claim must include a photograph of all original packaging components including box, inserts, and wrapping as received.
Claim for first-use defects on opened products must include video evidence demonstrating the manufacturing defect.

SECTION: PROCESSING TIMELINES

Return request must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.
Evidence review and acknowledgement shall occur within 1 business day.
Seal inspection shall occur within 1 business day of product receipt.
Claim decision shall be issued within 3 business days of product receipt.
Refund initiation shall occur within 2 business days of approval.
Refund credit to payment source shall occur within 3 to 7 business days depending on the bank.

SECTION: LEGAL OVERRIDE

Statutory consumer rights override platform policies.
Statutory rights pertaining to defective goods are not excluded by this policy.
